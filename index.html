Lawyers makes us feel safe, and helps us to not become screwed. Still, there are no way to know if the lawyer screws you.

Working as an entrepreneur friendly venture capitalist (VC), it hurts to see all money spent on pretty much standard procedures and contracts such as Term sheets, Shareholders agreements, Employment agreements etc., money that could have been used to create value for the startup. This is why we want to increase transparency to help founders improve their legal fees.

Never get screwed by a lawyer again:

  • Compare rates and quotes to market average and best in class
  • Track price development for all types of fairly standard procedures and contracts
  • contract.vc is free if you share you price points (100% anonymously).
  • It will also be secure, private and easy to use.

FAQ

What kind of contracts will be included?
All startup related contracts and procedures, but we will start with term sheets for seed and A-rounds.
Will contract.vc always be free?
contract.vc will always have a free tier for users that share data.
Is it possible to access the info _before_ negotiating with my lawyer?
Yes, you can get access by uploading the quotes that you recieve, but we will have incentives for uploading the real price point later on as well.
How do you secure the quality of the data?
We require a scanned invoice for each price point. We are starting with price, but we will expand to the possibility to rate the overall experience from 1-5 in three categories later on.
Love the idea! How can I help?
1. Spreading this site on all kinds of social media and networks 2. Provide us with historical price points that we can use as seed data for the service (please send the invoce or qoute of each price point to contracts@contract.vc). 3. Help us with funding 4. Volunteer to help us promote and administrate the site (please send your application to volunteer@contract.vc)
I have historical price points for term sheets, do you want them?
Yes please! We want as much seed data as possible before we launch the service. Please send the invoice for each price point to contracts@contract.vc.
I do not know how to use Bitcoins, can I support with USD?
You can exchange Bitcoins instantly here: https://coinbase.com. However, we can transerf your USD for you if you send an e-mail to funding@contract.vc
How will you use the funding?
Hire two developers for three months, and then operations for 1 year.
When will you launch v1.0 of contract.vc?
It depends on how fast we get access to the needed funding and seed data. We believe it is possible with a beta release in October and a v1.0 in December.
